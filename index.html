Life Insurance

Life is full of uncertainty, so it’s important to plan for the unexpected. Life insurance is one of those things that’s better to have and not need it, than to need it and not have it. Patelco offers Life insurance through TruStage1, where you can select the policy that’s right for you.

Whole Life offers lifetime coverage that can help pay final expenses and more, whereas Term Life provides coverage that can help protect your family when it’s needed most.

Life insurance can be easy and affordable, and you’ll feel confident that your loved ones are protected.
